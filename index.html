Our Story

How We Met

It was a warm Sunday afternoon after Mass when our paths first crossed. Abigail was volunteering at the church bake sale, and Jerome couldn't resist stopping by for some of her famous banana bread.

What started as a simple conversation about recipes turned into hours of talking about our faith, families, and dreams for the future. We quickly realized we had grown up just a few neighborhoods apart but had never met until that moment.

Over the next few months, our friendship grew through youth group activities, shared service projects, and long conversations after evening prayers. God's hand was clearly at work in bringing us together.

The Journey of Our Sacramental Union

1

Entrance Procession

The wedding party enters the church, symbolizing the journey of life that the couple will now walk together. The bride is traditionally escorted by her father, representing his blessing and the transfer of care to her new husband.

2

Liturgy of the Word

Scripture readings that speak of God's plan for marriage, love, and family life. Typically includes an Old Testament reading, Psalm, New Testament reading, and Gospel passage.

3

Exchange of Vows

The couple publicly declares their intention to enter into marriage and exchanges wedding vows. In Catholic tradition, it is the couple themselves who confer the sacrament of marriage upon each other through these vows.

4

Blessing and Exchange of Rings

The rings are blessed as a sign of fidelity and enduring love. The circular shape symbolizes eternity, with no beginning or end, just as the couple's love should be.

5

Nuptial Blessing

A special prayer asking God's grace and blessing upon the couple's marriage. This beautiful prayer highlights the sacredness of the marital bond.

6

Lighting the Unity Candle (Optional)

The couple each takes a candle representing their individual lives and together lights a third candle, symbolizing their new life as one in Christ.

7

Offering Flowers to Mother Mary

A traditional Indian Catholic practice where the couple places a bouquet of flowers at the statue of the Blessed Virgin Mary, seeking her intercession for their marriage.
